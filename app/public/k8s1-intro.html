Introduction to Kubernetes

Kubernetes is an open-source platform for automating deployment, scaling and management of containerised applications. It orchestrates container clusters, handling tasks like deploying containers, scaling based on demand and automating rollouts and rollbacks. Kubernetes simplifies the management of containerised applications by handling the underlying infrastructure, enabling efficient resource utilisation and providing declarative configurations for deployments.


To learn more about Kubernetes and how to get started, visit the Kubernetes "Getting Started" website.
